FOR OUR FARMERS

An initiative to help and uplift the unsung heroes of our country, our dear farmers. We at Bayanihan para sa Magsasaka firmly believe that people who till the land and feed us should never go hungry.

We started as a COVID-19 donation drive initiative to help farmers who were greatly affected by the pandemic. For our Farmers PH, Inc. is a youth-led, non-stock, non-profit organization which aims to help and uplift local farmers and fisherfolks.

OUR

MISSION

We aim to help and uplift Filipino farmers and fisherfolks through sustainable and inclusive projects towards a more productive agriculture sector of the country.

OUR

VISION

We aspire and envision positive changes in the state of life of our farmers and fisher folks -- better opportunities, better income and for them to finally get the recognition they deserve and no longer be called the unsung heroes.

OUR WHY AND PHILOSOPHY

We aim to help and uplift Filipino farmers and fisherfolks through sustainable and inclusive projects towards a more productive agriculture sector of the country.

We believe that we can achieve bigger things through working hand-in-hand with partner individuals, organizations and businesses. With collaborative partnerships, we were able to extend our help to more farmers all over the Philippines.
