We believe the internet is a combination of good and broken and one of the broken parts of the internet is fake news that can harm people, the community, and even the whole country.

For our Farmers learning center is a safe space and learning facility built for all children in the farming communities in San Carlos City, Pangasinan. We believe that children need a better facility to learn especially during this online and modular setting. One thing that we noticed is children were immersed early in the use of gadgets and parents were not fully educated on how social media can potentially harm their children. When children spend their time mostly at home and in front of a gadget they do believe all the things they see on social media even the things that can harm them (chatting to strangers, bullying, pornography, etc).
