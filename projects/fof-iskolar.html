Frequently Asked Questions

How long will I sponsor my scholar? Is there a 'lock-in' period?

You may sponsor your scholar as long as you are able and willing.
There is no lock-in period.

We, however, require sponsors to notify us at least a month before the last provision of stipend to his/her scholars. We will post again the profile of the scholars in the candidate database, to find new sponsor for him/her.

Who are the candidate scholars?

Young farmers and students from the agri-fishery communities from all over the Philippines.

We prioritize students who are truly and greatly in need of financial support for their studies.

How to become a sponsor?

  1. Message us and provide your email address. We will send the database of candidate scholars in a form of short interview.
  2. The sponsors can then choose their preferred scholar & we will facilitate the linkage between the scholar & the sponsor.

Can I contact my scholar?

Yes, you may contact your scholar. However, all communications must be directed to us.

This is to ensure the safety and privacy of both the sponsors and scholars.

Will I get an update about my scholars?

Yes, you will receive a monthly update. All pertinent documents will be uploaded in the Google Drive link. These documents include report card, photo-documentation and monthly letter of your scholar.

Where can I view the candidates for scholarship?

Please send us a message or email us at forourfarmersph@gmail.com if you’d like to view the candidate scholars’ database & start supporting your own scholar.
