FOR OUR FARMERS

We started as a COVID-19 donation drive initiative to help farmers who were greatly affected by the pandemic. For our Farmers PH, Inc. is a youth-led, non-stock, non-profit organization which aims to help and uplift local farmers and fisherfolks.

HOW TO DONATE

SUPPORT US

We help local farmers and fisherfolks by giving them grocery packs, term life insurance, hermetic technology bags which they can use to improve the shelf life of their product and water-bill subsidy for a month.
